Abstract

We present Neural Shell Texture Splatting, a novel approach that enhances texture fidelity while reducing the number of primitives in neural 3D rendering. By integrating shell-based sampling with Gaussian splatting, our method balances geometric efficiency and visual quality, achieving state-of-the-art results on multiple benchmarks.
